About Me

I'm Erin, a first year ICS student at Camosun curently living in my second home: Sweden. I love to travel and experience new things to the point where all of my income goes towards plane tickets. I'm also a beast of a cook, my repertoire is gigantic and daily variety is what I strive for. As you can probably tell by this website, I really like cats. My own cat's name is Cream, and she lives with my parents back in Victoria. When I was a kid, my dream was to be a veterinarian so I could be with animals all day. I've strayed a bit from that goal now but creating a website with random cat themed things I found on Google probably makes up for it. There are a few other hobbies I have that I'm really passionate about, like reading fantasy novels, playing indie video games, and listening to Scandinavian power metal. I love anything that can transport me into a completely different world, no matter the medium.

Some fun facts about me:

  2. I've legally changed my name a total of two times
